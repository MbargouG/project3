About Me - section By Bianca Suchite

The beauty of a photograph lies within the story it silently tells, demanding attention and admiration from those who choose to listen. Each of Chicago based photographer, Mbargou Gueye’s, photos holds a complex story different than the last. His photographs immediately intrigue and provoke thought from its viewers. Claiming the world as his studio, Gueye creates art within whichever environment he chooses. Through the deliberate and strategic manipulation of color and contrast, Gueye pushes artistic boundaries through his photography, capturing raw emotion and making profound statements. At 27 years old, his artistry is still unfolding and evolving as he looks to continue to grow his platform and craft.

As with many beautiful things in this life, Gueye did not seek photography; instead photography found him. Initially purchasing a camera to document the fitness journey he underwent in 2016, Gueye soon realized both the power of the camera and his own artistic capabilities and chose to pursue photography professionally. Since then, his interest in the craft has blossomed and become his passion. His presence on social media has allowed Gueye the opportunity to establish his public platform and widen his reach, also allowing him to draw inspiration from the creative communities he comes across digitally. Gueye has every intention of continuing to improve his career, and intends on seizing such endeavor in 2019 through shooting more videos, hosting gallery events and pushing his clothing brand, ‘G U EYE’. As his career and artistic ability continues to elevate, Gueye’s photographs continue to speak volumes to the world.
